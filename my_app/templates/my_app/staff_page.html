About Us

We have Trusted Housesitters, a global, pet-loving community on a mission to create a world.

This house-sitting arrangement requires the house sitter to provide regular visits to the home owner's house.

House sitting has evolved from its original purpose of local sitters helping regional pet owners, to a much wider network of international house sitters who travel the world looking after other people's homes and pets. The industry is supported by a number of professional international and regional house-sitting websites that match house sitters with homeowners around the world following the principles of the sharing economy, whereby pet- and home-care services are provided in exchange for free accommodation.
